About Anacruse

Biography

I'm Afonso Fonseca, vocalist and guitarist of Anacruse. I create videos about things and subjects I love to interact with people who like me and the work I do.

As the founder of the band Anacruse, I explore electronic and experimental sounds, always seeking to innovate and surprise my audience through compositions and live performances.

On my YouTube channel, I share content about music, gaming, and things I'm passionate about. I also love games and stream almost daily, connecting with my community through gaming sessions.

Journey

About Anacruse

Biography

Content creator, musician, and developer passionate about technology and art. Anacruse combines programming experience with passion for music, creating unique content that connects these two worlds.

Founder of the band Anacruse, Afonso explores electronic and experimental sounds, always seeking to innovate and surprise his audience through his compositions and live performances.

On his YouTube channel, he shares knowledge about development, music, and technology, always with a didactic and accessible approach for all experience levels.

About Anacruse

Biography

Anacruse is a rock band led by vocalist, composer, multi-instrumentalist, streamer and influencer Afonso Fonseca. Founded in 2007 in the capital of Rio de Janeiro.

As the founder of the band, Afonso Fonseca seeks the sound of Rock with external and multicultural influences, always trying to have his own identity but at the same time always diversify.

On his channels and social networks, Afonso interacts directly with his audience, sharing about his musical career, his daily life, content about his interests, music, games and a lot of spontaneity and irreverence. Doing lives almost daily, always trying to connect with his community.

Journey

2004

First steps learning instruments, playing drums self-taught, guitar and keyboard.

2005

His first shows, mainly in cover bands, primarily playing drums.

2007

Foundation of the band and their first shows.

2008

Entry into the professional music circuit.

2009

Recording and independent distribution of various acoustic demos like "Fale-me" and "Me Divertir, Me Distrair".

2013

First official band demo with all instruments, the demo "Me Divertir, Me Distrair".

2014

Creation of the official Anacruse channel on YouTube.

2015

Release of the first album "Apenas Meu Show".

2016

Release of the first official music video, "Me Divertir, Me Distrair" and the foundation of Afonso Fonseca's official channel.

2019

Beginning of Afonso Fonseca's live streams, initially only on the Twitch platform.

2020

Release of the singles "Acelerar/Bom Sofrimento", produced by Afonso Fonseca himself.

2023

Release of the second album "Dialeto Musical".
